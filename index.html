Silang Cabag Cave

If exploring is your idea of relaxation, you can also opt to explore Cabag caves, found in the neighboring town of Maragondon. Inside the cave is a 50-meter (164-foot) long river, where fresh, clear water flows into a brook. The valley where the cave is located is surrounded by lush greenery. The beautiful setting, coupled with the sounds of nature can do wonders to help calm your mind.

Gourmet Farms, Inc.

Gourmet Farms, one of the first organic vegetable farms in the country, offers consumers a wide variety of vegetables and herbs, including but not limited to lettuce, rosemary, thyme, parsley, and tarragon, all of which are grown in the 11.5-hectare (28.4-acre) land. However, it is not just a purveyor of goods. The land is open for eco-tourism, with visitors not only able to tour around the property but also learn about the different ways that they themselves can grow their own organic produce. Visitors can also take lessons in cooking, salad making, and recycling.

Ilog Maria Honeybee Farm

Ilog Maria Honeybee Farm is a haven for those who love natural remedies, all of which are organic, and most honey-based. Ilog Maria Honeybee Farm cultivates Italian honeybees, which are known for choosing the ripest pollen in flowers.

Buffy's Milk

Those who have a sweet tooth but want to be a little bit more careful about their diet may want to try out Buffy’s Milk, a dessert café that serves only all-natural products. Carabao (water buffalo) milk is used in all their dairy products and milk-based sweets.

Balay Indang

ood health is not just about nutrition. Equal focus should be given to mental health. A lot of good will come out taking the time to take a breather at Balay Indang, which is found in the municipality of Indang, a neighboring town of Silang. Each room in Balay Indang is uniquely decorated with simple yet beautiful wooden furnishings. The grounds are filled with trees and plants, perfect for soul seekers, or for those who simply want a quiet reprieve from their busy lives. Meals are inclusive in the package price.

Cornerstone Pottery Farm

Sometimes, choosing a quiet hobby is all it takes to still your mind. At Cornerstone Pottery Farm, you can try pottery making to take a break from you busy lifestyle. Cornerstone Pottery Farm also offers ready-made earthenware and ceramics for functional purposes, or simply for décor.

Saujana Premiere

Saujana Premiere is more than your average spa. Aside from the usual features, such as body and facial treatments, a sauna, and jacuzzi, it also offers alternative and complementary treatments such as traditional Chinese medicine, craniosacral therapy, autogenic training, acupuncture, detoxification, Ayurvedic healing, and even packages for anti-aging, wellness, and nutrition. Western medicine is also offered here, including but not limited to cardiology, dermatology, endocrinology, dentistry, plastic and reconstructive surgery, and physical rehabilitation with hydrotherapy. The 16-hectare (39.5-acre) village also comes with its own eco-trails, where guests can take a hike. Group activities, such as tai chi, are also offered.

Balinsasayaw Restaurant

Silang is home to a variety of restaurants that offer good food. One of the most well-known there is Balinsasayaw, which serves traditional Filipino dishes. Aside from good food, visitors from outside the Philippines will be able to experience Filipino culture in this restaurant. Guests are seated in nipa huts, the traditional homes of the country. The Balinsasayaw restaurant overlooks a picturesque view of the Taal Volcano, so those who choose to dine here will also be able to enjoy the beautiful surroundings.
